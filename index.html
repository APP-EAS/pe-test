Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How long does the coverage process take?

Your news will be published across our media network within 5-7 business days from submission.

Are the placements guaranteed?

Each plan includes a set number of guaranteed features on top-tier outlets. Beyond these, we distribute your news to hundreds more reputable publishers, though their acceptance may vary. You’ll always receive a detailed report of all guaranteed placements.

What kind of news can I place?

Company news, product launches, business milestones, appointments, and other newsworthy business announcements can be placed.

Do you help with writing?

While our focus is on securing coverage, we’re happy to offer editorial guidance and feedback on your press release. This helps ensure it resonates with journalists and audiences worldwide.
